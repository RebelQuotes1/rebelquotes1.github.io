Top 10 Bug-Out Bag Essentials

#1: Water Filtration – LifeStraw or Sawyer Mini keeps you alive. Period.

#2: Emergency Blanket – Lightweight warmth that saves lives.

#3: Ferro Rod – Fire doesn’t care about your lighter’s fuel.

#4: Multi-tool – Fix gear, cut cord, open cans. A must.

#5: First Aid Kit – If it bleeds, you better stop it.

#6: Paracord – Tie, trap, drag, lash. Infinite uses.

#7: Flashlight – Preferably solar or hand-crank.

#8: Knife – Fixed blade. Sharp. Reliable.

#9: Duct Tape – You know why.

#10: N95 Mask – Smoke, dust, disease barrier.

Stay ready. Die slow.
