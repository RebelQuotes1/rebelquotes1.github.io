Top 10 Cold Survival Items

Because frostbite is a poor man's cremation.

  1. Wool Blanket – Not that fleece garbage. Real wool, or freeze.
  2. Hand Warmers – Like hope in your gloves. Temporary, but better than frost-nip.
  3. Firestarter Kit – Lighters die. Ferro rods don’t whine in the cold.
  4. Mylar Sleeping Bag – Space burrito or hypothermia. Choose wisely.
  5. Layered Clothing – Cotton kills. Go synthetic or go cold.
  6. Thermal Socks – Because losing toes is bad for morale.
  7. Cold Weather Gloves – Fingers matter. Don’t donate them to winter.
  8. Waterproof Boots – Wet + cold = game over.
  9. Portable Heater – If you can carry it and fuel it, it’s survival luxury.
  10. Whiskey – Not recommended. But understood.
