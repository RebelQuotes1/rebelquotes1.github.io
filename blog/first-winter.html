My First Winter Off-Grid

I thought I was tough. The cold had other plans.

Water froze in my thermos. My boots froze to the floor. I cursed everything. And then I figured it out.

I learned the hard way: prep smarter, not just harder. Cold doesn’t negotiate. It erodes you slowly.

I made it out. Barely. And now, I overprepare for every winter. Because cold is cruel, but predictable.
