Top 10 Pieces of Survival Gear

  1. Multitool – Because duct tape can't cut rope.
  2. Headlamp – For when both hands are busy and the dark is hunting you.
  3. Water Filter – Because cholera isn't vintage anymore.
  4. Paracord – From tent line to tourniquet. Magic rope.
  5. First Aid Kit – Pain is mandatory. Infection isn’t.
  6. Knife – Don't leave home without it. Ever.
  7. Firestarter – Flames solve most problems.
  8. Emergency Blanket – For warmth, signaling, or wrapping up regret.
  9. Field Guide – Because nobody memorized everything.
  10. Attitude – Seriously. Tools are nothing without mindset.
