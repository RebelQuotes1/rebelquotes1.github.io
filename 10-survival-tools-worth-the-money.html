  4. 5-Day Survival Backpack – Pre-packed and ready for grab-n-go panic.
  5. Tact Bivvy 2.0 – Reflects body heat like a toaster oven on high.
  6. LifeStraw Mission – For groups or dirty river missions.
  7. UCO Stormproof Matches – Light even after a dunk in a swamp.
  8. Baofeng UV-5R Radio – Cheap but powerful. Talk to your crew when the towers drop.
  9. Leatherman Wave+ Multitool – Fix your world with your pocket.
  10. Bear Spray – For bears, tweakers, or anything with teeth.

Don’t trust your life to dollar-store junk. If it can’t survive a drop, dunk, or dirt bath — it’s dead weight.
